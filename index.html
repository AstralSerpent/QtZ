quan-tize

Verb. Apply quantum theory to, in particular restrict the number of possible values of (a quantity) or states of (a system).

Quantize (styled as QtZ or QuantiZe) is an insanely fast-paced, competitive twitch shooter based on ioquake3.

Easy to learn. Hard to master. The skill ceiling is defined by the players, not the game. With a huge focus on speed, movement tricks, ungodly weapon skills and dominating the arena, only the determined will stand a chance.
